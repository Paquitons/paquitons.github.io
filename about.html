About Iron Volt Electric

Powering Houston communities with professional electrical work built on experience, honesty, and craftsmanship.

Our Story

Built on Experience. Driven by Trust.

"Iron" stands for durability and strength — quality work that lasts. "Volt" is electricity itself. Together, they represent what we deliver: solid, reliable electrical work that Houston homeowners and businesses can depend on.

Over the years, Iron Volt Electric has grown into a trusted partner for residential homeowners, commercial businesses, local parks, and neighborhood developments throughout Greater Houston. Our team brings hands-on experience and a commitment to doing the job right the first time, every time.

We’re not a call center or a franchise. We’re your local Houston electrician — familiar with local codes, local homes, and the Houston climate’s demands on your electrical system.

How We Work

Our Core Values

Six principles that guide every job we take on — from a simple outlet fix to a full commercial rewire.

Safety First, Always

Every job is performed to NEC code with no shortcuts. Electrical work done wrong is dangerous — for your family, your property, and for us. We don’t compromise.

Proven Expertise

Over 25 years of hands-on experience in Houston homes and businesses. We’ve seen it all — old knob-and-tube, modern smart home systems, and everything between.

Radical Honesty

Transparent estimates, clear communication, and honest recommendations — even when it means telling you that you don’t need the more expensive option.

Community First

Proud to serve Houston’s neighborhoods, parks, small businesses, and local institutions. We live and work here — this community is our home too.

Uncompromising Quality

We use quality materials and take our time to do the job right. A rushed or shoddy electrical job can cause fires or failures years later. That’s not how we work.

Your Time Matters

We show up on time, work efficiently, and keep your home or business clean when we leave. Your schedule is respected — not treated as an afterthought.

Our Promise

Backed by Our Guarantee

Satisfaction Guarantee

Every job we perform is backed by our satisfaction guarantee. If something isn’t right after we’ve finished, contact us and we’ll return to make it right at no additional charge. No runaround, no excuses.

Code Compliance Guarantee

All Iron Volt Electric work is performed to current NEC standards and all applicable local codes. We pull the required permits, coordinate inspections, and ensure every job passes. If it doesn’t, we fix it — period.
