About Iron Volt Electric

Our Story

Built on Iron.
Powered by Volt.

With 25 years of hands-on experience in the electrical trade, Iron Volt Electric was founded with a simple goal: build a team of skilled, licensed electricians that customers can truly trust.

The name says it all — "Iron" symbolizes strength, durability, and dependability. "Volt" represents the energy, solutions, and power we bring to every single project.

Over the years, Iron Volt Electric has grown into a trusted partner for both residential and commercial clients across Houston — combining decades of expertise with a commitment to safety, precision, and service that keeps properties powered, protected, and running smoothly.
