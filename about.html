About Iron Volt Electric

Powering communities with professional electrical solutions — built on experience, trust, and craftsmanship.

Our Story

Built on Iron.
Powered by Volt.

With 25 years of hands-on experience as an electrician, Francisco Fierro founded Iron Volt Electric with a simple goal: build a team of skilled, licensed electricians that customers can truly trust.

The name says it all — "Fierro" means iron in Spanish, symbolizing strength, durability, and dependability. "Volt" represents the energy, solutions, and power we bring to every single project.

Over the years, Iron Volt Electric has grown into a trusted partner for both residential and commercial clients across Houston — combining decades of expertise with a commitment to safety, precision, and service that keeps properties powered, protected, and running smoothly.
